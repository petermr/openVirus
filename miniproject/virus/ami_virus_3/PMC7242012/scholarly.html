Summary

SARSâ€�CoVâ€�2 is a new human pathogen, readily spread via respiratory droplets and the cause of the growing COVIDâ€�19 pandemic. Men, older adults (&gt;65Â years old), and persons with comorbidities are at risk for severe COVIDâ€�19 and death. Countries have successfully suppressed transmission and mitigated needs for intensive care with robust case/contact detection (testing), isolation of infected/exposed persons, and social distancing. To stop the COVIDâ€�19 pandemic, the challenge is for all countries to scale up and sustain prevention measures while awaiting the arrival of effective vaccines and therapies.
